## Naming: reckies vs recs

The table PRODUCT.md calls **`reckies`** is named **`recs`** in the live Supabase database,
for compatibility with the web app at myreckie.com. The two names are synonymous — treat
every reference to a "reckies table" as `public.recs` in all future work. Do not rename the
table.
